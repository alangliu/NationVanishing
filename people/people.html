Reverend Suamalie
Our Identity

Rev. Suamalie NT Iosefa is the leader of a Tuvaluan church in New Zealand. The migrant Tuvaluan community has purchased 10 acres of land on the outskirts of Auckland to be turned into a retirement village and nursery.

Suamalie hopes to preserve the Tuvaluan language, culture, and identity for future generations, whether or not Tuvalu continues to exist.
