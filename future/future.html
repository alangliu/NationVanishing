The Need for Change

The plight of the pacific islands serve as a warning to the rest of the world. Immediate international action targeting climate change reduction is a necessity to securing the future.

A Global Issue

The Pacific islands are surrounded by water where rising tides and catastrophic storms serve as a reminder of the dangers of human impact on the environment.

They are not alone. Half of the world's population lives within 60km of the sea. If temperatures continue to increase and fossil fuel consumption goes unchecked, the story of the Pacific islands could be the story of us all.
